# HLSMonitor Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 5, 2026

HLSMonitor is a diagnostic tool for HLS video streams, published by
Kindly Ops, LLC. It is built so that your data stays on your device.

## What we collect

Nothing. HLSMonitor has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no
crash reporting, and no servers of its own. We never see your stream
URLs, your monitoring results, or anything else about how you use the
app.

## What stays on your device

- **Stream URLs you enter** are used to load the stream and, if you turn
  on "Remember URL", are stored only in the app's local storage.
- **Monitoring sessions** (segment counts, download times, quality
  incidents) are recorded on the device and kept for 7 days so you can
  generate quality reports. They are never transmitted.
- **Audio loudness metering** — whether from the inline player or the
  optional "HLSMonitor Loudness" screen-broadcast extension — computes
  loudness levels (LUFS/dB numbers) in real time. No audio or video is
  recorded, stored, or uploaded.

## What leaves your device

- **Network requests to the stream you choose to monitor.** The app's
  built-in browser loads the URLs you enter, exactly as any web browser
  would. Those requests go to the stream's servers and are subject to
  those services' own privacy policies.
- **PDF quality reports you share.** Reports are generated on the device
  and go only where you send them through the iOS share sheet.

## Children

HLSMonitor is a professional tool, is rated 17+ because of its
unrestricted web access, and is not directed at children.
